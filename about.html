The Story Behind 27 Signatures

27 Signatures is a 3D rendering of a collection of signatures obtained during a trip to NYC in 2021. This website contains a 3D rendering of a sphere with all of the signatures collected on the baseball as an interactive experience. It's been some time since the baseball was originally collected, however I have filled it in with the recordings and memories I have associated with each signature.

This project aims to blend technology with personal memories, creating a unique digital representation of a physical memento. Each signature on the virtual baseball tells a story, capturing a moment in time during that memorable NYC trip.

Explore the interactive 3D sphere to discover the signatures and the stories behind them. The gallery section provides a more detailed look at each signature and its associated memory.
